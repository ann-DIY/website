I am currently a college senior at the University of Texas at Austin intending on starting the Physician Assistant Master's Program at the University of Texas Medical Branch located in Galveston. Physician Assistants (PAs) are licensed to practice medicine and provide a wide range of healthcare diagnostic and treatment services with physician supervision. PAs conduct physical examinations, diagnose and treat illnesses, order and interpret tests, counsel on preventive health care, assist in surgery, and write prescriptions. PAs specialize in areas like emergency medicine, internal medicine, surgery, pediatrics, etc. after completing a core group of educational requirements.
